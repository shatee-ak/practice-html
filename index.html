As I sit outside under an old plane tree that I know very well, I get the sense that summer is slowly coming to a close. A feeling accompanies this realisation.
A sinking, uncomfortable feeling that I know all too well.
It is the same feeling that comes on Sunday evenings as another week This feeling has been with me in one way or another for most of my adult life.
Now, as I sit in my favourite place under this old tree I can feel it again. The days are getting shorter and colder. Soon the trees will be bare.
I canot quite put my finger on it but it feels like the sands of time are running out.
There is a hollow feeling in my chest.
